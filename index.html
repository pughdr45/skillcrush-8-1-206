My first project was for a friend of my wife, they ran JADE Realty Group, Inc. I started out helping setup their computer system, network and accounting software. Their acconting software was not designed for property management so I created a custom database application for them. Their website was my first venture into web design. JADERealty.com is still active but I no longer monitor or update it.

Next came Half Pint Ranch. My neighbor and best friend moved back to his home state of Colorado where he bought a 65-acre ranch east of Denver. He raised miniature Hereford cattle, goats, sheep, chickens and a few pigs. I built his website HalfPintRanch.com to advertise his cattle, goat milk & cheese and fresh eggs. The website is not longer active but you can view a copy at HalfPint.

I currently volunteer for the Orlando Ski and Travel Club as their webmaster, OrlandoSkiandTravelClub.com. They use Wild Apricot, a membership management software. Wild Apricot host their website and provides a WYSIWYG editor. I use HTML and CSS to improve and add features. Check them out at WildApricot.com.
